## Location

La Fortuna de San Carlos is in the northern lowlands of Costa Rica, at the base of the Arenal Volcano — approximately 3 hours from San José. It is one of the most visited destinations in the country, famous for its rainforests, waterfalls, hot springs and incredible wildlife.
