# Rain Forest Experiences CR

> Personalized nature tours in La Fortuna, Costa Rica — led by certified naturalist guide Gabriel.

## About

Rain Forest Experiences CR is a local tour company based in La Fortuna de San Carlos, Costa Rica. Founded by Gabriel, a certified naturalist guide with a Bachelor's degree in Ecological Tourism and over 13 years of experience leading tours in the Arenal Volcano region.

We specialize in small-group, personalized nature and wildlife tours. Every tour includes round-trip hotel transportation, professional optical equipment (spotting scopes, binoculars), water and snacks, and the guidance of an expert naturalist.

## Tours Offered

### 1. Night Rainforest Walk — $70/person
- Duration: 3 hours
- Difficulty: Easy
- Start time: 5:00 PM (daily)
- Minimum: 2 adults
- Description: Discover the nocturnal world of the rainforest. Observe the famous red-eyed tree frog, sleeping toucans, camouflaged insects, venomous snakes (safely observed from a distance), and more. The walk follows an easy, wide, and safe trail — approximately 1.5 hours of gentle walking through the forest.
- Includes: Round-trip hotel transport, certified naturalist guide, park entrance fee, water & snacks, flashlights & optical equipment.

### 2. Sloth & Wildlife Tour — $70/person
- Duration: 3 hours
- Difficulty: Easy
- Start times: 8:00 AM, 2:00 PM (daily)
- Minimum: 2 adults
- Description: A guided safari through the lush rainforest to observe sloths in their natural habitat, along with howler monkeys, toucans, poison dart frogs, and tropical birds. Walk approximately 2 km on flat terrain, cross 2 hanging bridges, and enjoy a tropical fruit snack with a view of Arenal Volcano.
- Includes: Round-trip hotel transport, certified naturalist guide, park entrance fee, tropical fruit snack & water, optical equipment.

## Location

La Fortuna de San Carlos is located in the northern lowlands of Costa Rica, at the base of the Arenal Volcano. It is approximately 3 hours from San José and is one of the most visited destinations in the country, known for its rainforests, waterfalls, hot springs, and incredible wildlife.
